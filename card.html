Horse

Horses can sleep both lying down and standing up.

  • Scientific Name: Equus caballus
  • Habitat: Land
  • Life-Span: 25-30 years
  • Number of Teeth: 36-40

It was domesticated from wild horses by humans at least 5000 years ago. They are large, strong animals, and some breeds are used to pull heavy loads. ... A male horse is a stallion, and a female horse is a mare. The general term for a young horse is foal.
